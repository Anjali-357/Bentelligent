    • Your Role

    Department and Positions

    • Role 01

      Management Team

      As a member of the Management Team, you play a central role in shaping and enhancing the extracurricular experience for your fellow students. Your responsibilities extend across various aspects to ensure the success and smooth functioning of the diverse range of clubs on campus.

    • Role 02

      Design Team

      As a member of the Design Team, you play a crucial role in bringing visual creativity and aesthetics to various club activities and events. Your responsibilities extend across creating engaging visual content, promoting club initiatives, and contributing to the overall branding and identity of the clubs.

    • Role 03

      Social Media Team

      As a member of the Social Media Team, you play a vital role in enhancing the online presence and engagement of the clubs. Your responsibilities include crafting compelling social media content, managing platforms, and fostering a vibrant online community.

    • Role 04

      Technical Team

      As a member of the Technical Team, your role is pivotal in ensuring the smooth execution of various technical aspects related to club events and initiatives. Your responsibilities encompass handling technical setups, troubleshooting issues, and contributing to the seamless integration of technology into club activities.

    • Role 05

      Content Team

      As a member of the Content Team, your role revolves around creating compelling and engaging content that communicates the club's objectives, activities, and achievements. You play a vital role in shaping the narrative of the club through various forms of written, visual, and multimedia content.

    • Role 06

      PR and Outreach Team

      As a member of the PR (Public Relations) and Outreach Team, your role is to enhance the club's visibility, build positive relationships with various stakeholders, and effectively communicate the club's achievements and initiatives to the wider community.
